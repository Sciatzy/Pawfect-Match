Our Adoption Process

We've designed our process to ensure the best matches between pets and their new families. Here's what to expect:

Browse Available Pets

View our gallery of adoptable animals and read their profiles to find potential matches for your lifestyle.

Submit Application

Complete our online adoption form with information about your home, experience, and what you're looking for in a pet.

Application Review

Our team will review your application within 2-3 business days and contact you to discuss next steps.

Meet & Greet

If your application is approved, we'll schedule a time for you to meet the pet at our facility or a foster home.

Home Visit

For some adoptions, we conduct a brief home visit to ensure your environment is suitable for the pet.

Final Approval

Once approved, we'll finalize the paperwork and schedule your adoption day!

Adoption FAQs

What are your adoption fees? +

Our adoption fees range from $50-$300 depending on the animal's age, species, and any special medical care they've received. All pets are spayed/neutered, vaccinated, and microchipped before adoption.

How long does the process take? +

The entire process typically takes 1-2 weeks from application to adoption. Some cases may move faster if all requirements are met quickly.

Can I adopt if I rent my home? +

Yes! We just require written approval from your landlord and may verify pet policies for your rental property.

What if the adoption doesn't work out? +

We offer a 14-day trial period. If for any reason the adoption isn't working, we'll take the pet back and refund your fee (minus a $25 processing charge).
